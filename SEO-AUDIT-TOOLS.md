## Duplicate Intent / Doorway Risk Watchlist

These pages should remain monitored because their intents are close. They are not recommended for noindex today, but they need distinct examples and wording over time.

- 	ools/ai-tweet-generator/ vs 	ools/x-post-generator/ - similar output intent.
- 	ools/unit-converter/ vs 	ools/length-converter/, 	ools/weight-converter/, 	ools/temperature-converter/ - parent/child converter intent.
- 	ools/readability-score-checker/ vs 	ools/readability-seo-checker/ - overlapping readability analysis intent.
- Formatter family pages such as 	ools/html-formatter/, 	ools/css-formatter/, 	ools/javascript-formatter/, and 	ools/json-formatter/ - similar format but different data types.
- AI generator family pages - useful, but should keep tool-specific examples to avoid a generated-directory feel.

## Pages Needing Manual Content Later

- `tools/ai-tweet-generator/` and `tools/x-post-generator/` - add platform-specific examples and clarify whether both are needed long term.
- `tools/unit-converter/`, `tools/length-converter/`, `tools/weight-converter/`, `tools/temperature-converter/` - add more formula/unit-specific examples over time.
- `tools/readability-score-checker/` and `tools/readability-seo-checker/` - add side-by-side examples so intent remains distinct.
- Formatter/minifier family pages - add code-language-specific before/after examples when possible.
- AI generator family pages - add audience-specific prompt examples for the highest-traffic tools.

## Recommendation

Keep all 150 tool pages indexable for now. Prioritize manual expansion for the watchlist pages and the top traffic candidates first. If Google Search Console later shows crawled-not-indexed patterns for the watchlist, consider merging, canonicalizing, or noindexing the weakest duplicate-intent page.
