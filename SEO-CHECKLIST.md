# ToolVanta SEO Checklist

- Confirm every important page has a unique title and meta description.
- Confirm canonical URLs match the final domain.
- Submit `https://toolvanta.space/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
- Inspect the home page, all category pages, top resource pages, and top tools.
- Keep internal links natural: tools should link to related tools, categories, and resource guides.
- Add real AdSense units only in clearly labeled ad slots.
- Avoid popups, fake download buttons, and misleading calls to action.
- Periodically improve tool pages with examples, use cases, and clearer FAQs.
- Track indexed pages and search queries in Google Search Console.
