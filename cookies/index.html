Cookie Policy

This policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on ToolVanta, including analytics and advertising cookies.

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Necessary cookies

Necessary cookies support basic website behavior, security, consent choices, fraud prevention, or technical delivery. ToolVanta's core browser tools are designed to work without account cookies, but some browser or hosting features may still rely on essential storage.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies or similar technologies may help ToolVanta understand aggregate visits, page performance, popular tools, and navigation patterns. This information helps improve site quality without requiring user accounts.

Advertising cookies

ToolVanta uses Google AdSense. Google and advertising partners may use cookies to serve ads, measure ad performance, limit repeated ads, detect invalid activity, and personalize ads where allowed. Advertising cookies are controlled by browser settings, consent options where available, and Google's advertising settings.

How users can manage cookies

You can manage cookies in your browser settings by blocking, deleting, or limiting third-party cookies. You can also use privacy tools offered by your browser, device, or advertising providers. Blocking some cookies may affect advertising, analytics, or saved preferences, but ToolVanta's core static tools should remain accessible.

Contact information

Cookie questions can be sent to support@toolvanta.space or through the Contact page.
