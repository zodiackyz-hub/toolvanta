# Deployment Checklist

- `.nojekyll` exists in the root.
- `index.html`, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, and `manifest.json` exist in the root.
- `assets/css/styles.css`, `assets/js/main.js`, `assets/js/tool.js`, and `assets/js/data.js` load with relative paths.
- Tool pages open with `/tools/tool-name/`.
- Resource pages open with `/resources/page-name/`.
- Mobile navigation opens and closes correctly.
- Browser console has no critical JavaScript errors.
- The final ZIP contains the site files at the root, not inside an extra nested folder.
