Privacy Policy

ToolVanta is built as a static, browser-based tools website. This policy explains what data may be processed, how cookies and advertising work, and how to contact us.

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Information processed by browser tools

Most ToolVanta tools run directly in your browser. Text, numbers, colors, images, JSON, prompts, and other tool inputs are processed locally by your device unless you choose to copy, download, print, or share the result elsewhere.

Analytics usage

ToolVanta may use privacy-conscious analytics to understand aggregate page usage, popular tools, referral sources, device categories, and performance signals. Analytics data helps us improve navigation, fix issues, and prioritize useful content. We do not use analytics to create user accounts or collect tool input values.

Cookies usage

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. ToolVanta's core tools do not require login cookies. However, cookies or similar technologies may be used for essential site behavior, analytics measurement, advertising, fraud prevention, frequency capping, and remembering privacy choices where supported.

Google AdSense and advertising cookies

ToolVanta uses Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and personalize or limit ads depending on your settings and applicable law. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and manage ad personalization through Google's own privacy and ad settings pages.

Data collection and user rights

Because ToolVanta has no login system and no backend database for tool inputs, we do not maintain user profiles for normal tool usage. Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or information about personal data that may be processed through contact messages, analytics, or advertising partners.

Contact information

For privacy questions, requests, or corrections, contact ToolVanta at support@toolvanta.space or visit the Contact page.

Trust and editorial purpose

ToolVanta is designed with minimal data collection in mind. The core tools run in the browser and do not require accounts. Advertising and analytics providers may use cookies according to their own policies, but ToolVanta does not maintain a backend database of normal tool inputs.
